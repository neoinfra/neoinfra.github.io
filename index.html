Neo Paradise

Neo paradise

Neo paradise is a collection of premium villa plots spread across 25 acres, of which, 10 acres in phase I, approved by BMRDA (APA/LAO/25/2014-15). Phase II of 15 acres, is under approval process.

Neo Paradise is located on the rapidly developing Jigani Aanekal road. It's close enough to the bustle of bangalore's IT HUB Electronic city and Bannerughatta Road. It is right beside Surya City which is spread over 3000 acres and right behind DLF Woodland Heights.

It is only 12 kilometers from Silk Board and surrounded by STRR and Radial Ring Roads. Neo Paradise is in close proximity to some of the finest schools, colleges, hospitals and shopping malls.
